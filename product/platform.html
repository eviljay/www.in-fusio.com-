Gamezilla's goal is to supply a single, coherent interface for the complete range of economic actors involved in Connected Mobile Games, from telecom operators to cellular phone manufacturers, development studios, game and player animation services.

This interface is designed to adapt to every technology currently available, whether the games are embedded in the mobile, downloaded (via ExEn®) or played on a remote server and connected via ExEn®, SMS, GSM data or GPRS data channels. A Web site is also connected to the Game Center. In this way, players have all their favorite titles on the Web and can continue to play or improve their skills using an Internet connection. The site also provides other practical and freely accessible information, such as the contests currently in progress, scoring, prices and billing, etc...

The various databases store all the information required to serve each and every actor. For example, they transmit statistics to telecom operators who can thereby measure and keep track of the audience and success of a game on the basis of the billing. On their side, players can download additional higher game levels or send in their best scores, win prizes and so on.
