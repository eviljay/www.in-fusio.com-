Football Fans / SPORTS / Wap
   
  An on-line derivative of the very successful Fantasy games so popular in the UK and now invading the rest of the world. Be the manager of your own football team: select a squad of real soccer players, set up the team before fixtures and gain points according to the performance of each of your players in real life. Participate in a global league or make up your own private league to play with your colleagues and friends.
