Recto Verso / CLASSICS / Wap
   
  A minute to learn, a lifetime to master! Recto Verso (face off) is a game with reversible pieces, played on a checkers board. Each player takes turns to place one piece on the board. Any of his opponents' pieces situated between his latest piece and others he has already placed on the board are then turned over. You win when you have the highest number of pieces on the board at the end of the game.
