Jurassic Park III / ARCADE / ExEn® / Wap
   
  Universal Pictures, Steven Spielberg’s Amblin Entertainment and In-Fusio have joined forces to bring you an authentic arcade/action dinosaur game based on the Jurassic Park III movie. Relive the excitement and the adrenalin rush of the movie as you try to escape dinosaurs in a succession of chases through labyrinths. On foot, by car, plane or boat, you had better keep going because your pursuer is right on your tail! Your only goal is to finish the game in one piece!
