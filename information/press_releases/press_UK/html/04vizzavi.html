In-Fusio provides WAP games for Vizzavi pan-European portal

In-Fusio, pioneer and leading supplier of connected mobile game solutions to telecom mobile operators signed an agreement for WAP games with Vizzavi Ltd., the new multi-access European portal.

In-Fusio announces the recent launching of two popular games for Vizzavi, the rapidly growing 50/50 joint venture between Vodafone Group plc and Vivendi Universal.

The two WAP-based games are Football Fans, a fantasy football game, and Quizz, a general knowledge game. Both have been available to members of the Vizzavi UK portal (www.vizzavi.co.uk) since December 2000. They will soon be available on the other Vizzavi portals in Europe.

Football Fans has been developed exclusively for Vizzavi and lets a player manage his own football team. To set up a fantasy team, the manager has to select 11 football players from any of the Premier League clubs. The manager's fantasy team will then gain points according to the real performance of each of his football players. It's like the real thing - the manager can even do player transfers.

Quizz features four general knowledge categories: Art, Science, History and News. The questions are asked at random with answers chosen from a multiple choice. If a player gets three correct answers in any category, he wins a golden coin. Collect a golden coin for every category and a bonus of 10 golden coins is awarded. Each week, players are ranked according to their scores and the winner is the player with the highest score.

A complete game concept

In-Fusio delivers a full service:

  • Hosting, enabling thousands of simultaneous game sessions with the GameZilla platform
  • Statistics, instant data for the billing, the track of audience and success of each game
  • Animation, contest organization at regular frequency, winner reward with high value prizes
  • Management of Internet sites, game description and scoring available on the web site

About Vizzavi

Vizzavi is a 50/50 joint venture between Vodafone Group, the world's leading mobile communications company and Vivendi Universal, Europe's leading media company. Vizzavi is a multi-access portal designed to provide services to customers in a consistent format, throughout Europe, across different platforms, including mobile handsets, personal computers, television and personal digital assistants. Vizzavi was established in July 2000 and currently operates PC and mobile internet sites in France, the Netherlands and UK. For more information on Vizzavi please go to http://www.vizzavi.net

About In-Fusio

Founded by Gilles Raymond in August 1998, In-Fusio pioneered the launch of games on mobile phones. As both designer and developer, In-Fusio is the first company in the world to offer connected mobile games to several operators in Asia, Europe and Australia, and has now managed over 2 million game sessions played.

Right from the start, In-Fusio stood out from conventional start-ups with its international vision in terms of both customers and co-workers: 11 different nationalities are already represented among the 70 employees present on-site in Bordeaux, France, as well as those working in their design studio in Shanghai.
