In Fusio opens its capital to Banexi Ventures II

Bordeaux, France: September 21rst, 1999

In Fusio, a French company specialized in games, contents and services development for mobile phones, opened its capital to the venture capital company Banexi Ventures II.

In Fusio is a young innovative start-up set up one year ago. Thanks to its prospective market vision the company has built since successful partnerships with multinationals such as SFR, Schlumberger, Packard Bell Europe, Mannesmann and T-Mobil.

With SFR, In Fusio realized a world-wide unprecedented event while launching games in WAP (Wireless Application Protocol : inside mobile phones navigator).

With Schlumberger the company also carried out another unprecedented event by developing games on SIM Toolkit.

The latest agreements, in particular with T Mobil (mobile phone subsidiary of Deutsch Telecom) confirm the world-wide leading position of In Fusio on entertainment for mobile phone.

The entrance of Banexi Ventures II in companyís capital will be a significant asset to reinforce its growth, allowing In Fusio both to develop its offer and its international coverage.

The engineers team, managed by Sebastien BRUHAT cofounder of In Fusio, combines the control of mobile phone advanced technologies (WAP, SIM Toolkit) to original and innovating games resulting of this young company creativity.

Mobile phone will be the over the air interactive Walkman of video games consoles", enhances Gilles RAYMOND, In Fusio's CEO and cofounder.
