In-Fusio revolutionizes Mobile Gaming

As a world first, In-Fusio delivers its ExEn® -based games to Orange. This product launch is the first step in a large-scale deployment of this mobile game downloading application.

Bordeaux, July 3, 2001 — "The future is not in five years time, but it is today, and what we are doing today with In-Fusio is simply revolutionary" publicly announced Guy Lafarge, Executive Marketing and Communication Director of Orange France. Hot on the heels of downloadable ring tones, videogames can now be sent directly to your mobile phone!

Ten months after announcing its revolutionary ExEn technology, In-Fusio, pioneer in games for cellular phones, has delivered to Orange a whole catalogue of downloadable video games, launched this week (see the attached the list of games).

Created and developed by In-Fusio, ExEn® (Execution Engine) is an application embedded in the terminals, allowing the user to download games in complete freedom. ExEn today provides the best real-time video interface, also managing sound, colour and 3D animated graphics. Uniquely dedicated to gaming, it uses eight times less memory, yet runs twelve times faster than other general-purpose virtual machines. Once downloaded, In-Fusio games are played on the phone, without the need for a permanent network connection.

ExEn can be installed in most types of cellular phones and runs on existing networks and billing systems without any upgrades. According to Giles Corbett, Managing Director of In-Fusio, this is one of the key arguments in favour of this technology: "ExEn games are attractive to operators, because they offer a perspective of short-term revenues, in line with the philosophy of a market tainted by the disappointment with WAP. After the launch of our first games in April 2000, we saw a usage rate of 64% among cellular phone owners! To date, no other application has achieved such results."

Towards a standardized technology for all players in the mobile gaming world.

Sold on the quality of ExEn's graphics and animation, the game studios Kalisto and Cyronetworks are collaborating with In-Fusio in order to offer new products to the mobile gaming world. One of the games, Tinies, created by Kalisto, is ready and already included in the launch catalogue.

Thanks to ExEn, big names in entertainment are also open to games for cellular phones. The licensing agreement recently signed by Universal Studios and In-Fusio has resulted in the animated game Jurassic Park III, which follows on directly from the famous dinosaur saga. This game is available to operators and is included in the initial games catalogue .

In-Fusio entered into a decisive phase last May when it announced the development of a standard JavaÔ programming interface (API) for mobile games with Motorola, the second largest manufacturer of cellular phones in the world: "The expertise and the experience acquired by In-Fusio with its ExEn technology and by Motorola with JavaÔ will simplify the development of games for mobiles and impose the reality of this new market, available to application developers," stated Gilles Raymond, CEO and founder of In-Fusio.

While waiting for this new interface and in order to meet the expectations of the players in the market, In-Fusio is pursuing its deployment of ExEn.

In the coming months, no fewer than four manufacturers (of which two have already been announced, Philips and Trium) will have integrated ExEn into their mobile phones.

After the world premiere with Orange, several major European and Asian operators will proceed with the launch of ExEn games this summer.

Ten In-Fusio games presented by Orange under ExEn Technology

See demos on www.in-fusio.com !

JURASSIC PARK III: Universal Pictures, Steven Spielberg’s Amblin Entertainment and In-Fusio have joined forces to bring you an authentic arcade/action dinosaur game based on the Jurassic Park III movie. Relive the excitement and the adrenalin rush of the movie as you try to escape dinosaurs in a succession of chases through labyrinths. On foot, by car, plane or boat, you had better keep going because your pursuer is right on your tail! Your only goal is to finish the game in one piece!

WALL BREAKER: The original game. The aim is to clear levels of bricks by destroying them with the help of a ball bouncing off a pad controlled by the player.
With 100 levels to clear before you finish, the game is a real challenge for beginners and experts alike!

DECA CHALLENGE: A multi-event sports game. The player must compete in 10 sporting events (100 meter dash, long jump, archery, 110 meter hurdles, rowing, canoeing, javelin, swimming, 800m, shot put) to beat records and set the highest score. For each event, the player has to qualify in order to continue. This is the first mobile connected game to offer a multiplayer option (4 players).
Have you got what it takes to be the best?

TINIES: This is a game developed by Kalisto, the famous French game studio. Guide your Tiny and, thanks to its gas, make it land on a landing pad. Any collision with the environment will be fatal for you.

CRAZY PET II: Help this cute little mouse escape from the cats and eat the maximum number of pieces of cheese. A tip: magic pills are spread around the labyrinth allowing the mouse to eat the cats, but only for a while... You can renew your game by buying new levels.

MELI-MELO: Find the pairs, Méli Mélo is a memory game. The cards are lying face down and you must find matching pairs to eliminate them and finish the game. Keep an eye on the time, otherwise you will lose points.

HOROSCOPE: A funny and interactive horoscope? Yes, it is now possible! Choose your sign and play the 4 games, each related to a special theme (Love, Luck, Work and Health). You will then receive your horoscope for the week. Information is updated weekly. Watch out, you may become addicted !

PILE UP: Don't let the bricklayer build his wall! You are competing against a crazy guy who is throwing piles of bricks at you. The only way to prevent him building the wall is to arrange the bricks in such a way that entire lines disappear. A game in the "Oldies but goodies" category.

SKATE KID: Skate kid is a one player skateboarding game. The player has three lives to finish each level and must jump and crouch to avoid upcoming obstacles and enemies. He must also perform tricks to earn more points. So let's hit the sidewalk!

DREAM QUEST: Once upon a time, a young boy had a dream…In this adventure, you take on the role of this young hero. You have to escape from the Dream Castles you find yourself in every time you fall asleep. Move through the rooms in the castles, find items, keys, meet characters and fight against enemies to find your way out of each castle.

About In-Fusio, the Mobile Game Connection

The pioneer and specialist in Connected Mobile Games, In-Fusio offers connected games and their related services, such as organizing contests, managing players’ community, hosting statistics, etc. Today, In-Fusio has 300,000 regular players and 10,000,000 games played.

In-Fusio’s offer is based on a growing portfolio of over 20 games and the development of its ExEn technology and Gamezilla platform.

ExEn® is the very first technology dedicated to downloading games over-the-air while offering the very best video interface for real time animated graphics. Two of ExEn’s major assets are to be integrated onto entry-level handsets and run over today’s networks.

Gamezilla’s goal is to supply a single, coherent interface for the complete range of economic actors involved in the "Mobile Game Connection", from operators to cellular phones manufacturers, and development studios, to game and player animation services.
