Gilles RAYMOND C.E.O.
Gilles Raymond founded In-Fusio in 1998, bringing to the team both his knowledge of the gaming industry and his extensive sales experience. The very first person ever to conceive the idea of creating connected games for cellular phones, he developed the business model and proved that the concept worked by obtaining contracts with SFR, France Telecom, T-Mobil, BT Cellnet, Sunday and Telstra. Prior to founding In-Fusio, Gilles worked for Psygnosis, Sony's video game development branch. He also co-founded Digitug, the European distribution company for Corbis CD-Roms (a Bill Gates enterprise).
gilles.raymond@in-fusio.com
Giles CORBETT Managing Director
Giles Corbett joined In-Fusio in April 2000 to structure the company as it moves into its next phase of development. He brings experience in managing fast growing environments and an understanding of operators' needs in Value Added Services and marketing. Thanks to his experience as Marketing Director of Vizzavi France and Management Consultant for Arthur D. Little in London, Giles was a key contributor to the success of the second round fundraising for In-Fusio. He is currently concentrating his efforts on both strengthening In-Fusio's international expansion as well as its product development, in order to reinforce the company's strategic position in the connected gaming market. Giles received his BSc in Economics and Political Science from the London School of Economics and his MBA from INSEAD in Paris.

Leon Van de Pas VP Sales
Léon Van de Pas joined In-Fusio in March 2000. He is currently responsible for signing contracts with mobile operators, internet portals, and distributors, and is in the process of building up the sales teams.
