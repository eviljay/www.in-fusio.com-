In-Fusio spirit
The In-Fusio team is currently over 70-strong, covering 11 nationalities, who have all joined to carry out the mad task of making this young Bordeaux-based start-up the world leader in Connected Mobile Games. The company's values are Fun, Respect, Confidence and Innovation.
Combined with a strong team spirit, a large dose of company-wide ambition and clear individual missions they guarantee that however hard the work, we all have a good time.
