OUR BUSINESS
"In-Fusio is producing games for cellular phones that are genuinely fun for all thanks to its ExEn® technology" Gilles Raymond, C.E.O., In-Fusio
 
 
   
 
  The pioneer and specialist in mobile connected games
   
 
 

Created in 1998, In-Fusio was the first to launch the concept of games for mobile phones. The company provides telecom operators with connected games and their range of related services, such as hosting statistics, organizing contests, managing the player community.

Since its creation, In-Fusio has distinguished itself by its strong international vision and expansion. In-Fusio's team is currently over 90-strong, with members from a dozen nationalities. The company has reinforced its position as world market leader thanks to its considerable game portfolio, its download technology (ExEn®) and its Gamezilla platform. It has today 11 operators worldwide, including Vizzavi Europe, Terra Mobile, Orange, Sunday, China Mobile, T-Motion, Telstra™ etc..., and registers over 2 million game sessions played and paid for every month!

In November 2000, the company secured a second round of financing from important international investors, for the development of products such as the ExEn® engine, and for the company's international expansion.
