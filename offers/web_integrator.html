web integrator

As part of our Web Team, you will be responsible for the integration of our games onto our web site. This will be a process aimed at maximizing the possibilities offered by the web in order to add an extra dimension to the product we provide our clients

Principal duties and responsibilities:

  • Integrating the design concepts and content of our games in order to create web pages
  • Putting the newly developed web site online
  • Keeping abreast of new integration technologies and sharing this information with team members

Education, experience and skills required

  • You have some previous experience in web site development
  • You have strong HTML coding skills and are very familiar with the Dreamweaver authoring tool
  • Some knowledge of JavaScript, php and mysql would be a plus.
  • You are highly motivated to share and develop your knowledge and technical abilities
  • You possess very good interpersonal skills which will facilitate your integration into our Web Team

If you are a flexible, proactive individual and you are eager to participate in the growth of a highly international start-up company, then please send us your CV with a covering letter outlining what qualities you will bring to the job
