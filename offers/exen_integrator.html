integrator

As part of the current project to develop our own EXEN technology, you will be working in close collaboration with both our partners and the handset manufacturers

Principal duties and responsibilities:

  • Prepare the integration : meeting with the manufacturers in order to define the API's and the tools required to ensure that EXEN functions optimally.
  • Develop a simulator for the manufacturer and the EXEN adaptor.
  • Go to the manufacturer's site in order to integrate EXEN into the handset.

Education, experience and skills required

  • Masters degree (or equivalent) in computer science
  • Very good knowledge of C
  • You must also be familiar with the constraints enforced by the hardware and systems of both telephones and computers.
  • Ideally, you will have some previous experience in the telephone industry.
  • Knowledge of an object language (for example, Java) is a plus. As would be previous experience of embedded technology

You are adaptable, you can work autonomously and with rigor. You can work in a team and understand the importance of respecting our partners and aiding them in their development. You are really enthusiastic about working in a multicultural start-up along with a desire to succeed in this highly competitive industry
