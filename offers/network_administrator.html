network administrator

As part of our Research & Development department and directly responsible to the Head of Network Administration, you will be involved with the maintenance of our internal network

Principal duties and responsibilities:

  • Perform administration of a network running on Windows NT / 2000
  • Provide technical support and troubleshooting for the users
  • Management of our mailbox system under Microsoft Exchange

Education, experience and skills required

  • You have a computer or technically oriented training
  • You have some previous experience of network administration
  • You are exceptionally comfortable in a Windows NT / 2000 environment
  • You are familiar with the Microsoft Exchange server
  • You are a proactive problem solver with good interpersonal skills
  • Experience of an IP network would be hugely advantageous
