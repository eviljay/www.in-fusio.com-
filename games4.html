Push
 Place the ice-cubes at their final position. Not as easy as it seems at first glance when you know that you can only push them, and that the ground is icy here and there. A terrific puzzle game, especially at level 47. The minimum moves the penguin makes, the maximum points you get. You can buy new levels of game, register their high scores and win prizes if you're the best!
Supported technologies : connected (SMS) embedded game
