In-Fusio, the Mobile Fun Connection, is a high-tech gaming start-up providing fun for a mass market audience via communicating mobile devices.

Worldwide leader - we currently animate games for the customers of Itinéris (France Telecom's mobile subsidiary and market leader), SFR (first French private operator), T-Mobil (Deutsche Telekom’s mobile phone subsidiary), Sunday (Hong Kong operator)and Telstra (Australian market leader).

Our offer - ranging from simple card games to sophisticated video games, covers all commercially available communicating mobile technologies and is designed to make the most offuture technologies. Beyond game development, In-Fusio specialises in one-stop game design, provision, hosting and animation for its partners.

Created in August 1998, In-Fusio now has over 60 employees from 9 countries worldwide. The Company received its first round of venture capital financing from Banexi Ventures (affiliated with the BNP Paribas Group) and is in the process of seeking a second round of venture financing.
