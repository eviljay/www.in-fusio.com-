In Fusio, the Mobile Fun Company, is a high-tech start-up company providing entertainment content and services for a mass market audience via mobile phones. The offer includes development, hosting, launching, and animation of games using WAP, and SIM cards integrated in the mobile phone. Its aim is to use all the assets of the mobile phone as a new interactive, personnal media tool to meet the needs and wishes of end users.
 
Worldwide leader, In Fusio has already launched games in full deployement with SFR (first French private operator), T-Mobil (Deutsch Telecom's mobile phone subsidiary), France Telecom, BT Cellnet (the mobile division of British Telecommunications) and Sunday (Hong Kong operator). In Fusio has set up partnerships with Alcatel, Schlumberger, Mitsubishi…
 

In-Fusio, the inventor of interactive games via mobile phones, has developed two original games for France Telecom's new " Les Games " range
