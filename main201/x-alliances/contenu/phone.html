In-Fusio Joins Phone.com Alliance program 

Bordeaux, France - July 13, 1999 - In-Fusio, a high technology start-up company that specialises in entertainment content and services for wireless communication, has joined the Phone.com™ Alliance program as a content provider.

This marketing alliance is another example in the integration of Internet services, games, and wireless phone technology that is happening worldwide.

By utilising Phone.com's alliance program, In-Fusio will provide subscribers access to games and entertainment from their mobile phone.

 
In-Fusio Products
 
In Spirit and Quizz are applications that are available today for mobile phones.
  • In Spirit is a role-playing combat game that is offered to players around the world.
  • Quizz is a multiple-choice trivia game that covers subjects such as history, geography and entertainment.
