As world-leader in games and gaming technologies for mobile phones, we are currently expanding and seeking new talents to help us sustain our healthy growth. The company’s headquarters are in the beautiful and warm south of France (Bordeaux).

Integrator

Ref.RD0501IE/IF

As part of the current project to develop our technology ExEn, you will be working in close collaboration with both our partners and the handset manufacturers.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • You will participate in the development of our technology ExEn using C
  • You will work with and manage the constraints of an embedded environment (mobile phone)
  • You will participate in the integration : meeting with the manufacturers in order to define the API’s and the tools required to ensure that EXEN functions optimally.
  • You will identify and correct potential bugs
  • You will keep yourself updated on technological developments

Education, Experience and Skills required

  • You have a Masters degree (or equivalent) in computer science
  • You have very good knowledge of C
  • You must also be familiar with the constraints associated with an embedded environment.
  • Knowledge of an object language (for example, Java) is a plus. As would be previous experience of embedded technology.
