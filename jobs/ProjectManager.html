PROJECT MANAGER

RD0501PM/IF

Responsible for the entire project, you will manage both the creation and the development of our client/server platform. This platform represents the very core of what we do. You will be in charge of the planning, implementation and control of the entire project.

Main Duties and Responsibilities

  • You will lead and motivate our team of computer engineers

  • You will be in charge of the development of the platform with your team

  • You will be responsible for the successful and on-time delivery of the project

  • You will prepare written reports concerning all the work you do

  • You will put into place the processes which will ensure the success of the project

Education, Experience and Skills Required

  • You have a Degree or Masters in Computer Science

  • You have at least two year’s experience in the management of a client/server project and you are extremely familiar with Java

  • Knowledge of object oriented methodologies (UML) would be a plus
