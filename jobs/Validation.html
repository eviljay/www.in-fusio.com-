SOFTWARE ENGINEER : TEST & VALIDATION

RD0501TV/IF

You will participate in the creation and development of our games and technologies on mobile phones.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities

Working closely with the Test and Validation Manager, you will be responsible for following the test and validation process. Your tasks will involve :
  • Developing the test methodology
  • Setting up and writing the test documentation
  • Putting into place these tests in a technologically innovative environment
  • Harmonising the test and validation procedure
  • Tracking and managing errors and bugs
  • Testing and validating games which are developed either internally or externally

Education, Experience and Skills Required
  • You have a Bachelor’s degree in computer science
  • You have a very good knowledge of object oriented programming (Java)
  • A good understanding of RMI, EJB and distributed architectures would be a plus
  • Also, if you have previous experience of Oracle and SQL even as a user then this would be of added benefit.
  • You are rigorous, autonomous, you are highly sensitive to the importance of testing and have many investigation skills.
