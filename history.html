history
All you wanted to know about In-Fusio

October 00: Terra Mobile chooses
In-Fusio's games for its portal.

September 00: In-fusio launches its unique new Exen technology that allows players to download games into mobiles.

July 00: Phone.com teams with
In-Fusio to enable new bred of location based mobile games.

July 00: In-Fusio celebrates its
500 000th game session.

September 99: In-Fusio raises first round financing.

April 99: In-Fusio carries out the world's first full commercial deployment of games for mobile phones with SFR.

February 99: In-Fusio develops the first connected games for sim cards with Schlumberger.

October 98: In-Fusio trials the first connected games on mobile phones in the world with SFR (French Operator) using pre WAP technology.

August 98: In-Fusio is founded.
