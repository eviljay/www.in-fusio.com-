Everything you always wanted to know about In-Fusio
August 98 : In-Fusio is founded.

October 98 : In-Fusio trials the first connected games on mobile in the world with SFR (french operator) using pre WAP technology.

February 99 : In-Fusio develops the first connected games for sim cards with Schlumberger.

April 99 : In-Fusio carries out the world's first full commercial deployment of games for mobile with SFR.

September 99 : In-Fusio raises first round financing.

Today: In-Fusio has since established worldwide leadership offering games in France, Hong Kong, Germany, Australia and the United Kingdom. It is preparing for the future, trialling GPRS and animated 256-colour connected games.
