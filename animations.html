games animation
In-Fusio ensures the animation of all its games to increase the players' loyalty.
In-Fusio provides a complete service of games animation, including :
- the organisation of competitions : planning of contests, definition of scoring
- the animation of players' community : sending "push" messages, creating and animating a web site dedicated to the games
- the management of prizes : finding attractive prizes adapted to a special target of players
- the management of winners : sending congratulation messages, implementation of a toll-free number, reception and management of phone calls, sending prizes.
